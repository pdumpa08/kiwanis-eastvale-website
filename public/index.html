Who Are We?

Welcome to the Eastvale Kiwanis Club! We are a diverse group of Eastvale residents united by a belief that the children of our city need to be supported, valued, and offered a helping hand as they grow up. We invest in the success of the children in our community and strive to offer programs and opportunities that will help them realize their full potential. We hope you enjoy our website and if you’d like to learn more, we would be honored to have you attend one of our monthly meetings.
