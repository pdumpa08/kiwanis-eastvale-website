About Kiwanis

Kiwanis Eastvale is one of the largest volunteer organizations in our City. We are a diverse group of Eastvale residents united by a belief that the children of our city need to be supported, valued, and offered a helping hand as they grow up. We invest in the success of the children in our community and strive to offer programs and opportunities that will help them realize their full potential. We hope you enjoy our website and if you’d like to learn more, we would be honored to have you attend one of our monthly meetings.

A Salute to Service

On Feb 25, 2024, we will recognize our most active members with the President's Volunteer Service Award. This is a prestigious recognition awarded by the Government of the United States of America and is passed down to volunteers all over the nation through verified organization such as ours. Members who have served their community for a certain period of time over the past year are eligible to receive this award.
