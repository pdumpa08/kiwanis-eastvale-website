Kiwanis Projects

At Kiwanis Eastvale, we are dedicated to contributing to our community through collaboration, allowing us to foster developmental opportunities for members at every level. We are proud to partner with the City of Eastvale, JCSD, and numerous other youth and volunteer groups throughout our community to run our projects.
