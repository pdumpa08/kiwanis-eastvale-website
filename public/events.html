Kiwanis Events

At our core, we are a volunteering organization. We provide opportunities to work with various community groups including the City of Eastvale, Jurupa Community Services District (JCSD), Eleanor Roosevelt High School's Key Club, and more. Community service hours earned through these events determine members' eligibility to earn the President's Volutneer Service Award at our end-of-year banquet.
